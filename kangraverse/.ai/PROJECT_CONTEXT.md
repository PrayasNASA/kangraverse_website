# Project Overview

KangraVerse is an AI-powered Digital Heritage WebGIS platform designed to document, visualize, analyze, and preserve the living sacred landscapes of the Western Himachal Himalayas.

The platform combines Geographic Information Systems (GIS), Artificial Intelligence, community knowledge, cultural heritage documentation, and research visualization into a modern web application.

Unlike tourism websites or traditional map applications, KangraVerse is a research-driven platform that supports documentation, education, conservation, and community participation.

2. Understand relationships between communities and sacred landscapes.

4. Preserve oral traditions and community knowledge.

5. Support heritage conservation through digital technology.
